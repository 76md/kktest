Some common questions

What is Greeny Dips (Soups) ?

It’s a Dip Soup using tea-bag concept, 100% Vegan, 100% Natural and Preservative free. A spinach based product providing benefits of traditional food with maximum nutritional value. Our Spinach & Plan based products are tasty, easy to consume and can effectively be integrated as a part of modern diets.

Can office beverages be a part of workplace wellness programs?

Absolutely! Including office beverages as part of a workplace wellness program can encourage healthy habits. A common office beverage includes coffee, tea and sometimes soft drinks, which does not yield much towards a wellness lifestyle , however providing healthier beverage options such as our Dip-Soups can contribute to overall employee well-being.

Are these Dip-Soups a Beverage or a meal?

Our Dip Soups are developed as a Beverage keeping in mind how busy our world has become and how hectic a lifestyle current time demands. As a beverage, our dip-soups allows for portion control, nutritional hydration, easy to make & being extremely portable. Like a Coffee or Tea, our Dips Soups can be consumed anytime of the day/night, any season and by all age groups multiple times a day.

Can I offer these to my employees ?

Absolutely! More and more organisations have started focussing a great deal of their time on employee experience and wellbeing. In addition to current coffee , tea, green-tea culture, this dip-soup will revolutionize your beverage offerings for your employees & team. This is highly recommended to corporates, IT companies, Factories, Manufacturing units, Hotels , etc where high number of employees or guests are normally present.

Do I need to consider my employees dietary restrictions before ordering?

Some employees may have specific dietary needs or preferences. Our Dip-Soups are 100% Natural. Preservative-Free & Low-sodium and can be consumed by one & all. In the event an employee choose to avoid certain spinach or plants, we have 12 different varieties to choose from.

Is this product available for corporate cafeteria across India ?

Yes, our corporate pouches are available for delivery across India. Please contact us for more information on corporate deals and offers.
